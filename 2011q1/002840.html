Well regular Atari TT go above 14 MB (I think I've seen people using 
14+128 MB).

And on Falcon it's not just CT60 which have more memory; AB040, Mighty 
Sonic, Centurbo 2 (I might forget some) and on STe there is the PAK 030 
expansion which goes above 14 MB. Then of course clones go above as well 
(Medusa, Hades, Milan, ACP).

think doing it right from the start might be beneficial in the end :)
