--desktop option will force your Desktop resolution for fullscreen, so
there's no resolution change and therefore it should happen much faster
(especially with LCD monitors for which resolution switch seems painfully
slow).


If you had already max width & height set to your Desktop resolution, it's
possible (but not guaranteed) that this resolution switch didn't happen
earlier either.  It all depends what's your Desktop resolution and how well
Falcon resolutions, for the Atari stuff you've run with Hatari have used,
match that with integer scaling factor.
