Here is my patch to add IDE emulation in SDL-GUI and MacoSx Preferences 
panel:

- Show a IDE emulation CheckBox in the SDL-GUI (dlgdisk.c). If checked, 
fill according field in configurparams
- Force Reset in case of any modifications (change.c)
- Save IDE configuration (configuration.c) in preferences files.
- stuff for MacOsX -need to send also sdlmain.nib- to take care of IDE 
emulation, plus little other things.

Look like it's working.
