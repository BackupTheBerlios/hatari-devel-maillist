> As far as I can figure so far, GEMDOS HDD is the only drive unit not

Right.  IDE/ACSI drives are emulated at the HW level.  For GEMDOS drives,
Hatari hooks into GEMDOS within the emulation.


If you e.g. use something like:
	hatari -m --tos etos512k.img -d test/ --acsi hd.img

And hd.img has only one partition, you will see the ACSI drive partition as
C: and GEMDOS dir as D:.

(EmuTOS doesn't require a HD driver for HD images with DOS partition table)


However, there's something funny with the IDE & GEMDOS drive interaction
in current Hatari (not one having you GEMDOS drives patch).  For testing
your patch with the HD image stuff, you could use the ASCI emulation.
