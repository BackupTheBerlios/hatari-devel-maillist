Looks like you interlaced thed files in the wrong order. The hexdump of your file looks like this:

 06 2e 03 60 30 e0 00 00 00 e0 00 00 16 00 83 00
 30 e0 00 00 26 e3 60 00 91 24 19 09 38 05 17 00

But a real TOS image looks like this:

 60 2e 03 06 00 e0 00 30 00 e0 00 00 00 00 83 16
 00 e0 00 30 00 e3 60 08 09 24 19 91 00 03 17 38

The 0x602e is a "bra" opcode, the 0x0306 is the TOS version and the 0x00e00030 is the start address of the TOS code. Try to interleave your four files to start like this and it should work with Hatari, too.
