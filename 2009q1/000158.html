 Hi everybody!

I just wanted to announce that DSP emulation (everything that is not
related to sound) is running pretty good now, thanks to the great work
of Laurent Sallafranque!
Some things you might want to try with --dsp emu now:

- Running preview
- Bound3 demo
- Virtual City
- Fractal Flight
- Badmood
- Des Laser et des Hommes 2
- Papa was a bladerunner

Note: You need a very fast host CPU (> 2 GHz?) or you'll only see a
slide-show ;-)
