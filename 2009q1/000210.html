With a very fast search, I've found some programs or projects, but I 
don't know if one of them is useful for our goal, I'll try to have a look.



http://www.allegro.cc/ and you can get the latest stable build from 
http://alleg.sourceforge.net/
Basically you'll get back a raw audio buffer in an easy to use format 
and you can do anything to the audio that you could want, including play 
it back.

For recording and playback, cross platform, here's the one:
http://www.portaudio.com/

And here's another one:
http://www.music.mcgill.ca/~gary/rtaudio/ 
<http://www.music.mcgill.ca/%7Egary/rtaudio/>

Audio processing stuff:
http://clam.iua.upf.edu/
http://ccrma.stanford.edu/software/stk/
