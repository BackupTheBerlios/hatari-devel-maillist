> Hi,

> I need help for mercurial repos. I made a hg clone

> update the repository with hg status, hg update, or anything

Remember that when working with Mercurial, you hace a full copy
repository. THe first step is to update your repository with "hg pull".
Then you can update your working copy with "hg update".

When you do a commig, you will commit to your local repository. You must
propagate changes back with "hg push" or similar.
