On Nov 19, 2010, at 3:37 PM, Nicolas Pomarède wrote:

> Regarding the asm output, I really prefer this new presentation, "moveq" or "movem" are really easier to read for example.
> 
> There're 2 cases I'm less used to :
> 
> 1) adding paranthesises aroung absolute addr
> 	lea       ($ffff8907).w,a0
> 	move.l    d0,($2134e).l

That is the new Motorola way...


> I think it's more common to just have :
> 	lea       $ffff8907.w,a0
> 	move.l    d0,$2134e.l

This is what we used to use on the Atari.

Change:

static Diss68kOptions	options = doptOpcodesSmall | doptRegisterSmall | doptStackSP;

to:

static Diss68kOptions	options = doptOpcodesSmall | doptRegisterSmall | doptStackSP | doptNoBrackets;

to get this behavior.
