> 
> While we are speaking about VIDEL emulation...
> I made a small program which polls the video address counter to make 
> rasters. It works fine on Hatari in ST mode, but the rasters are not
> visible in Falcon mode.
> Do I miss something or is it one of the missing features on the VIDEL 
> emulation ?

Rasters are not emulated at all in Videl mode. Hatari currently renders
the whole screen with one palette only, you can not change the colors
inbetween. And of course you can also not change the screen adress or
resolution inbetween, that's why some games like Humans
(Evolution-Dino-Dudes) are not working yet. And HBL and Timer B are
running with the same frequency as the ST screen modes, the Falcon
screen timings are not taken into account yet.
