On Sat, 9 Oct 2010, Eero Tamminen wrote:

> What I meant is that if demo uses DSP, DSP emulation needs to be enabled in
> Hatari.  DSP emulation is so much slower than CPU emulation (whether CPU
> emulation uses JIT or not) that having JIT just for CPU does really help to
> speed up things.
>
> And I haven't heard of any JIT for Falcon DSP...

What we mean is that for _developing_ 060 demos, JIT CPU would be awesome. 
Todays max setting in Hatari is 040/32, which is heaps and bounds too slow 
to simulate something like a CT60.

To avoid DSP slowness, while developing one can always use a pure PCM 
soundtrack instead of MP2/ACE/MOD (it's easier to skip to a certain demo 
position then anyways). Add FastRam to that equation, and I'd say 
90% of the development could be done in Hatari :)
