Hello.

I use Hatari on Windows XP.
I noticed that the white color is not as bright as expected.

I made tests by writing the white value to 0xffff8240.

Falcon mode:
0xfff: full white -> OK

STe mode:
0xfff: light grey -> a bit too dark

ST mode:
0x777: darker light grey -> really too dark

Hatari should display a full bright white in all the cases above. And of 
course scale the intermediate colors accordingly.
