Thomas Huth wrote:
> I've added a "fast boot" option now - when disabled, the EmuTOS splash
> screen now shows up (you need also<= 4 MiB RAM for this because EmuTOS
> can not detect more RAM without memvalid patching).

Good !

But that's confusing, the "fast boot" is simply ignored when the RAM is 
set to > 4MB, I expected it would emulate a cold boot in that 
configuration, even if the OS detects only 4 MB (BTW EmuTOS should be 
improved to detect additional ST-RAM up to 14 MB).

Also, when the fast boot is disabled, Hatari always simulates a cold 
boot, even on reset. That's not good, Hatari should emulate a warm boot 
on reset. Or better: give the user to choose between warm or cold reset, 
just like Steem.
