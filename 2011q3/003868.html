On sunnuntai 31 heinäkuu 2011, Nicolas Pomarède wrote:
> Nevertheless, Paulo says that when in fullscreen the mouse can go at the
> bottom of the screen, but when he exits fullscreen, the mouse is
> "recentered" on a different Y position. Is there a reason for that, when
> going back to window mode, shouldn't the mouse position remain the same ?

I think that's because host mouse position compared to the Hatari window
position is different in fullscreen and in windowed mode and therefore
emulator gets (probably pretty large) mouse movement event from SDL
(which IKBD code splits into smaller -127 - 128 sized movement packets).

I could debug that at some point as Hatari window's position on my desktop
didn't seem to affect the mouse position offset on fullscreen switch i.e.
there's something suspicous with it, but that's not cause of the Hextracker 
issue...
