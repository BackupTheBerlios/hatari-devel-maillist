Well, according to Nicolas it's better in Hatari to rely on
the emulation CPU core[1] as it already has that information.
Instructions.py was more of a test. :-)

CPU core isn't maintained by us, but by Win/UAE and I don't know
whether they're planning any ColdFire support.  This kind of emulation
is concerned with cycle-level accuracy and ColdFire is fairly large
diversion from m68k, so I assume it would be a large change.  Does
anybody know whether this has been even discussed on UAE side?


[1] By default Hatari uses Markus' new disassembler, not the UAE
    core one though.
