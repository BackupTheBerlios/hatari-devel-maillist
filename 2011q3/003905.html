That will surely be useful when Hatari emulates a ColdFire CPU.

> Currently it's for example missing the floating point
> instructions (as they weren't in my book), are they same
> as on 68[346]0?

The ColdFire FPU supports only a subset of the original 680x0 FPU 
instructions, but the supported instructions are compatible.

If you actually need to know the encoding of all the 680x0 FPU 
instructions, the answer is there:
http://cache.freescale.com/files/archives/doc/ref_manual/M68000PRM.pdf

All the official documentation of the 680x0/ColdFire processors is 
freely available on Freescale's website. There are much more docs.
