Both filters are the same except mine is 4 times louder.
I want less round-off error and more speed so I divide by
four later while mixing the DMA and YM together.

Usually the filters are normalized so that the dc gain is one.
Leonard's filter is 1/4 + 1/2 + 1/4 = 1
Mine is             1   + 2   + 1   = 4
BTW the my previous filter was 1 + 1 + 1 + 1 = 4 thus has
a different frequency response.
