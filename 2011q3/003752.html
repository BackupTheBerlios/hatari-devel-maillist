It's about using the same resolution in fullscreen as what your desktop
has regardless of Atari screen size.  That avoids the multi-second delay
that LCD screens have from changing the resolution.

In the current code the --desktop option affects only Falcon/TT Videl
code as I had left it out from ST/STE mode duo to things I stated above.


I.e. the question is mainly which is more annoying, the delay on switch
(which on LCDs is much larger than on CRTs) and resulting skips in audio,
or Atari screen being at left top corner and not scaled (as resolution
isn't changed).


> at the moment, if I press F11, Hatari in
> STF mode is centered in fullscreen, I don't see anything wrong here ?

That's because it changes the monitor resolution.


With the attached patch, resolution isn't changed and switch to fullscreen
is instant.  Then you can use "--desktop no" to tell Hatari to switch
resolution.
