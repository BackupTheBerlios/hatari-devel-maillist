After trying it, in fact I'm not really convinced by the usefulness of 
this option in STF/STE mode : in my case, Hatari has zoom x2 (approx 
836x576 pixels) and my OS is 1280x1024.

When pressing F11, Hatari windows goes to top left, which leaves a lot 
of black area around, and with no offence intended, if no one told me 
before what to expect, I would have thought it was a bug. Calling an 
option "fullscreen" and filling the rest of the screen with black 
without actually zooming the Hatari's screen seems of little use to me.

Put a black wallpaper on your PC desktop and you will basically get the 
same result.

I agree that with extended resolution in TT/Falcon or VDI mode it can be 
useful if it really fills the screen, but for STF/STE, I think it adds 
more confusion than it solves problem.

People could be interested in a general (more complex to code) solution 
that allows full resizing of the Hatari's window, but I'm not sure this 
option would be used in STF/STE mode.
