Hello

I just updated the avi video recording to be able to support non integer 
frame rate, as produced by a real ST (and as we emulate them now)

For example, video produced by an STE in PAL should give 50.053 frames 
per sec, instead of the commonly used 50 fps. The difference is small, 
less than 0.1%, but it will be more accurate.

Avi header supports non integer fps, so the avi files produced by Hatari 
are following the specs and should not give playback problems (assuming 
you use a capable video player).

I tested it with "More Or Less Zero" by DHS, video and audio stay 
synchronised as intended during the whole demo, so I think this new avi 
version should be OK.

This required a change in the functions' parameters, please test it 
still compiles under OS X.


Nicolas
