> How do you unlock the mouse if you accidentally lock it and don't know

Mouse grab is disabled while emulation is paused, so just press Pause to
pause emulation or F12 for options.

There are no other Hatari AltGr shortcuts near the M key in normal keyboard
so it's unlikely that user accidentally presses this shortcut though.
