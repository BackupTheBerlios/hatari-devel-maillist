Eero Tamminen wrote:
> Does coldfire have new instructions?

Yes.

A few usermode instructions: mvz, mvs, mov3q...
Several supervisor instructions: cpushl, intouch, movec...
The MAC unit (Multiply and ACcumulate) instructions: mac, movclr...

The full reference is here:
http://www.freescale.com/files/dsp/doc/ref_manual/CFPRM.pdf
