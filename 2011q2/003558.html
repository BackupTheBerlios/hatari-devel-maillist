On keskiviikko 08 kesäkuu 2011, David Savinkoff wrote:
> I noticed that debugInfo.c addresses these languages:
> 
> "us", "de", "fr", "uk", "es", "it", "se", "ch" /* fr */, "ch" /* de */,
> "tr", "fi", "no", "dk", "sa", "nl", "cz", "hu"

OSConf values are actually supposed to be countries,
so I used (current) ISO country codes:
http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/ISO_3166-1_alpha-2#Officially_assigned_code_elements


> Note that EmuTOS addresses some in addition:
> 
> http://emutos.cvs.sourceforge.net/viewvc/emutos/emutos/po/
> cs.po
> de.po
> es.po
> fi.po
> fr.po
> gr.po
> ru.po

Whereas these are language codes.


> Maybe cs and cz are the same.

At least closely related.

However when TOS was created, CS was the correct two letter ISO
country name, see:
	http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/.cs

I changed this, then it's same as language code. :-)
