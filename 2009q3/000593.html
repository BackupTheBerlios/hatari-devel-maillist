Well, there were in fact two problems:

1) Hatari mixed the DMA sound samples and PSG sound samples with a bad
ration. This has been fixed with changeset e27e82952a01 and thus the
next public release 1.3.

2) Hatari does not emulate the STE microwire/LMC1992 chip yet, so if a
program tries to adjust the volume level, bass, etc., this won't work
in Hatari yet.
