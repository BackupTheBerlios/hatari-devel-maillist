David Savinkoff a wrote:
> I noticed that the sound buffering in hatari has a noticeable
> delay that is longer at low sampling rates. Just press a key
> and wait for the 'ding' to sound, try it at different sample
> rates on the ST or STE or any other setting.

I confirm this, tested on Hatari for Windows. The delay for the key click 
can be up to 1 second in 11 KHz.

This sound delay is the first thing I have noticed when I ran Hatari for the 
first time, and it is one of the main reasons I prefer using Steem, which 
has virtually no delay at all, while having crystal clear sound with no 
glitches.
