I decided to leave/add "experimental" for emulated HW features which
aren't very widely/extensively tested OR weren't in earlier releases.

I.e. the additions are for STE:
- Experimental Microwire/LMC1992 emulation

And for Falcon additions & changes are:
- Aspect correction and scaling of small resolutions by an integer
  scaling factor
...
- DSP co-processor emulation
- Experimental microphone (jack) emulation
- Experimental Crossbar sound matrix (ADC (mic & PSG), DAC, DMA, DSP)
  interconnect emulation + support for the additional DMA sound
  sample rates
- Experimental IDE master and slave emulation for harddisk support</li>

TT and midi/rs232/printer emulation have been in several releases, but as
they still aren't very extensively(?) tested, I left them as experimental.
