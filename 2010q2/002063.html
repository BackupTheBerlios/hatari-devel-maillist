Hello.

In the past, by making some test programs for Windows, I had noticed 
that requesting a playback at custom replay rate was not always honored, 
due to hardware limitations. For example, requesting a playback at 12 
KHz was leading to an actual playback at 11 KHz, or things like this.

I don't know if this oddity can affect Linux or SDL. But of course, it 
it was the case, it would lead to delays, etc.

However, 44100 Hz is the big standard, it is supported by all 
soundcards, so if there are delays in Hatari at that frequency, the 
problem is certainly unrelated to the issue I describe here.

Just in case...
