> No MP3 here, I'd like to know the real values and compare them with the

Z and Y are just two different notes, and with a fresh loaded ACE Tracker 
without any sound patch in memory, it shouldn't make any sound at all.

Plus I don't think you can get any reliable data from sampling a Falcon 
and comparing it with Hatari as there are so many factors involved.

Falcon: Analouge output, can be 32780 Hz, 44100 Hz, 48000 Hz or 49170 Hz, 
most often there is also a bass-boost in the Falcon. All of this will make 
different results if you sample the Falcon. Not to mention volume levels.

Hatari: Sound scaling from 32780 to 44100 (and all other combos) can also 
make datas unreliable.

I think you need a Falcon.
