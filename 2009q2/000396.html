Laurent Sallafranque <laurent.sallafranque at free.fr> wrote:

> During the loading of the "Alive demo", DSP is just loading the DSP
> code of the demo at address 7ea9 and more. It does nothing else. (no
> sound, no transfers to 68030).
> 
> So, I think that the picture with "Fun Industries" and a rotating
> wheel should display.
> (For me, it's just 68030 code, no DSP code).
> 
> (You can see the very first screen of the demo here : 
> http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jXZVhPX5OzA)
> 
> Do you think it could come from a Videl problem (a resolution that we 
> don't emulate correctly for example) ?

Videl emulation is still very basic, so this could be a problem. For
example there are no HBL raster effects emulated yet (you can see this
in the game Evolution Dino Dudes for example).
So if that demo switches some Videl registers (like the screen base
adress) more than once during a VBL, it will certainly fail to display
correctly.
Videl screen timings are also not implemented yet. HBLs and VBLs
are just triggered like in the normal ST low resolution (50 Hz
only ...).
