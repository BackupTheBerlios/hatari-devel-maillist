---> Original answear bu Thomas <----

That's normal, and not a bug. Hatari can not now about the exact
position of the ST mouse pointer, so it's quite impossible to always
sync it with the mouse pointer from the host. Hatari only passes
relative mouse movements to the emulated system, not absolute positions.
To overcome this problem, simply activate the "grab mouse" mode (by
pressing AltGr+m by default).
