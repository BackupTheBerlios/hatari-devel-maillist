Hi,

I've been for some time being slightly annoyed by logging/tracing Hatari
does.

So, I thought that whenever I see a message from Hatari to the console which
doesn't clearly and consistently identify from which Hatari subsystem it is,
I'll go and fix that.

I've now added "GEMDOS" and "IDE" identifiers to all messages logged
from gemdos.c and ide.c.

(This may help also when getting mails from users where they describe
what they do / what doesn't work as they think it should.)
