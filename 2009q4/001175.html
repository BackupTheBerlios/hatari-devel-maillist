On Wednesday 30 December 2009, LAURENT SALLAFRANQUE wrote:
> When in windowed mode, the mouse pointer is "offseted" in falcon mode.

Because of the issue Thomas explained, I added --grab command line
option to Hatari and made pause to do ungrab.

Just start Hatari with --grab and if you want to move the mouse outside
the Hatari window, pause Hatari (or press the key you've mapped to
the Mouse grab keyboard shortcut).
