Hello,

I just commited support for STE's specific left border's removal as used 
by DHS in their demos (More Or Less Zero Or Cernit Trandafir, also used in 
Save The Earth by Defence Force).

This method uses a hi/lo switch slighlty earlier than the usual 230 bytes 
method that works on STF and STE (but requires another hi/lo switch later 
to "stabilise" the resulting pixels shift)

Result should be close to a real STE, except for the 16 first displayed 
pixels that show 2 bitplanes under Hatari (under STE, this seems to 
completly show the background color, I'm not sure if it's the case ?).

While doing this, I noticed mixing STE's horizontal scrolling with 
overscan is displaying a 4 pixels block of background color at the right 
of the line, and the last pixels of the line are not correctly scrolled.
I will correct this soon (it was present before adding 224 bytes support).

Thanks to Anders (Evil/DHS) for running my test program under STE and 
sending me the resulting video.


Nicolas
