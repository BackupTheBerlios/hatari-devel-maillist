Hi!

I'm wondering if the NoCrew MP2-player is working now, as it does not 
work in 1.3. It's using a quite unorthodox method of transferring the 
MPEG stream to the DSP with sound DMA, and then the DSP sends it to the 
DAC after decoding. This effectivly gives the Falcon MPEG Audio at 0% 
CPU-load.

There are a bunch of demos using this replayer so it could perhaps be 
of interest to make it work..
