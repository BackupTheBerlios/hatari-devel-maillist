On Sun, 18 Oct 2009, Eero Tamminen wrote:

> What about the bootup case?
>
> Is it possible to delay Avi recording starting until bootup has progressed
> enough that the refresh rate is stable (i.e. to detect that somehow)?

Detecting this doesn't seem straightforward. But recording at 50 Hz 
while atari is at 60 Hz during the boot is not a problem in the end, because 
during the time atari boots at 60 Hz, only a white still image is 
displayed.

So, when playing the avi, you will only see a white image at the 
beginning, and you won't be able to see any problem, because this image 
doesn't "move", so it's not possible to see thata few frames don't have 
the exact fps (a white screen at 50 Hz looks the same as a white 60 Hz 
screen).
