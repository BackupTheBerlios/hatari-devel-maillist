Hi Anders,

Thanks for the sound files. I believe the sound bugs can be worked out
with these files as it is easy to hear what is going on.

The patch you applied is activated, the difference in the bass might
be heard if patched an unpatched versions of Hatari are compared.
The bass should be positively affected by the dc/subsonic IIR filter
because the previous FIR method is suboptimal; the 512 taps can only
address a cutoff frequency of 44100/512 = 86 Hz; running average
filters have bad frequency domain properties; and, and the delay
on a FIR filter is 512/2 = 256 samples!; and this delayed DC blocking
is applied to the current sound!

The IIR filter provided is fast(er?), and the perfectly correct method.

I want perfect sound, this filter is just one step forward.

David
